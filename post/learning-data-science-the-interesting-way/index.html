Learning data science-the interesting way

Learning data science can be daunting task. With all the coding and the bugs, you can be easily lose the motivation to keep on learning.There are times when you are going to spend hours or even days try to figure out how the code you need to solve a problem or fix a bug. You can easily give up on learning if you find the coding and the techniques to be difficult and challenging. So how can you keep yourself going and motivated when the going gets tough?Personally I have found learning data science skills to be interesting when you apply your data science skills to solve problems that interest you. Find out what interest you most?If your a football lover, learning data science will be more interesting to you if work on football-related data sets. Another way is to make learning more interesting is to solve problems in your domain. If your are in the health sector for example try to apply your data science skills to solve health-related problems.The same applies to those in the finance field. Those who are lucky to be in employement, you can make learning interesting by applying your skills in your daily work. Think of the of the problems that you face on a daily basis at your work place. Ask yourself how you can use your data science skills to solve the problems in your personal work, department or organization. Make use of the data lying in your work computer and start making a difference in your orgainization. The moment you begin to see the value of your data science skills in things that interest you, your domain or personal work , the more motivated you will be to learn more. Do not wait to amass a lot of data science skills before you start applying your skills in areas of interest. You will be surprised by the amount of data skills you will acquire in a short space of time if you start applying the skills in your interest areas.
